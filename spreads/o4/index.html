Put Credit Spread

View Details

Definition

Sell an OTM put & buy a lower-strike put to collect a net credit (neutral-to-bullish).

Benefits

  • Fixed maximum loss
  • Low capital requirement
  • High probability of profit (~60–75%)

Metrics

Max Profit
Credit × 100
Max Loss
(Width – Credit) × 100
Breakeven
Short Strike – Credit
Example: Underlying $52; Sell 1× $50 put @ $1.50, Buy 1× $45 put @ $0.50 → Credit $1.00 → Max Profit $100, Max Loss $400, Breakeven $49

Bull Call Spread

View Details

Definition

Buy a lower-strike call & sell a higher-strike call to pay a net debit (directional bullish).

Benefits

  • Limited risk
  • Lower cost vs. a single long call
  • Efficient upside exposure

Metrics

Max Profit
(Width – Debit) × 100
Max Loss
Debit × 100
Breakeven
Long Strike + Debit
Example: Underlying $52; Buy 1× $50 call @ $3.00, Sell 1× $55 call @ $1.00 → Debit $2.00 → Max Profit $300, Max Loss $200, Breakeven $52

Risk Management & Position Sizing

  • Risk 2–5% of account per trade
  • Take profits at 50–80% of max gain
  • Close positions ≥21 days to expiration
